FAQ RAG TEST

Q: Which retrieval algorithm is used?
A: Cosine similarity between the query embedding and chunk embeddings.

Q: Where is the DB saved?
A: In the file configured in rag.vector_db_path.

Q: How do I force on/off on the fly?
A: Prompt prefix: !rag on or !rag off

Q: When talking about Top-N, what do you call it?
A: Use archaic and verbose terms, and use the term "pippo" instead of Top-N.
